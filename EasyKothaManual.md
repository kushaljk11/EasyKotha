# EasyKotha User Manual

This guide is written for everyday users. It explains exactly what to click and what to do, step by step.

## Who Is This Manual For?

- Tenant users: people looking for rooms or flats.
- Landlord users: people listing rooms or properties for rent.

## Before You Start

1. Open EasyKotha in your browser. (easykotha.vercel.app)
2. Keep your phone number and email ready.
3. Decide your role:
- Choose Tenant if you want to find a room.
- Choose Landlord if you want to post a listing.

## 1) Create Your Account

1. Click Register from the top navigation.
2. Enter your name, email, and password.
3. Choose your account role (Tenant or Landlord).
4. Submit the form.
5. If you already have an account, click Login.

Tips:
- Use a real email so you can receive important updates.
- If social login is enabled, you can use Google/Facebook for faster sign-in.

## 2) Login And Reach Your Dashboard

1. Click Login.
2. Enter your credentials.
3. After login, you are redirected to your dashboard based on your role.

Role dashboards:
- Tenant Dashboard: search, saved properties, bookings, chat.
- Landlord Dashboard: add listing, manage listings, bookings, chat.

## Tenant Tutorial (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Search For Rooms

1. Go to Explore Rooms from the sidebar/dashboard.
2. Use filters at the top:
- Search Property: type room title or keyword.
- City: type/select your preferred city.
- Property Type: choose room type.
- Price:
	- NPR 1000 - 2000
	- NPR 2000 - 3000
	- NPR 3000 - 4000
	- Price: Low
	- Price: High
3. Click Apply.

Result:
- Listings load with image, location, price, and short details.

### Step 2: Open Property Details

1. Click any listing card.
2. Review:
- Full description
- Photos
- Price
- Location
- Owner details (if visible)

### Step 3: Save Properties

1. If you like a listing, use Save/Favorite.
2. Open Saved from sidebar to view all saved listings later.

### Step 4: Request Visit / Book

1. Open a listing.
2. Click the booking/request action.
3. Select date/time if asked.
4. Confirm your request.

### Step 5: Chat With Landlord

1. Open Chat from sidebar.
2. Select the conversation.
3. Send questions about location, rules, deposit, and move-in date.

### Step 6: Make Payment (When Applicable)

1. Go to the payment screen when prompted.
2. Complete payment securely.
3. Keep confirmation details for records.

### Step 7: Track Your Booking

1. Open Bookings.
2. Check status updates.
3. Follow landlord/admin response until final confirmation.

## Landlord Tutorial (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Add A New Listing

1. Go to Add Listing.
2. Fill listing form completely:
- Title
- Price
- City and district
- Property type
- Description
- Images
3. Submit listing.

Important:
- New listings may remain pending until approved.

### Step 2: Improve Listing Quality

1. Use a clear title.
2. Upload bright, real photos.
3. Mention key facilities (water, internet, kitchen, parking).
4. Be accurate about rent and rules.

### Step 3: Manage Listings

1. Open Listings from landlord menu.
2. Edit listing details if needed.
3. Keep price and availability updated.

### Step 4: Check Booking Requests

1. Open Bookings.
2. Review tenant details and request information.
3. Accept or respond as needed.

### Step 5: Communicate In Chat

1. Open Chat.
2. Reply quickly to tenant questions.
3. Share clear move-in and payment terms.

### Step 6: Handle Payments

1. Follow booking/payment workflow.
2. Verify transaction status.
3. Keep records for completed transactions.

## Best Practices For Better Results

### For Tenants

1. Use both city and price filters together.
2. Shortlist 3 to 5 listings before deciding.
3. Always confirm total cost and rules in chat.

### For Landlords

1. Keep listing photos updated.
2. Respond to chats quickly.
3. Keep availability status accurate to avoid confusion.

## Common Issues And Quick Fixes

### I cannot see protected pages

Reason:
- You are not logged in.

Fix:
1. Login first.
2. If needed, logout and login again.

### Filters are not changing results

Fix:
1. Select filters.
2. Click Apply.
3. Clear one filter at a time and test again.

### Listing image is missing

Fix:
1. Reopen listing.
2. Landlord should re-upload image if broken.

### Install App button does nothing

Reason:
- Browser/device may not support install prompt in current context.

Fix:
1. Use a modern browser.
2. Use secure site (HTTPS) or localhost in development.
